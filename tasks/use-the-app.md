# Using the My Favorite Albums App

*A Guide for Music Fans*

## Audience

This guide is for anyone who wants to explore album ranking data through 
the My Favorite Albums web app. You do not need to install anything or 
know how to code.
---

## Getting to the App

Open any web browser, or platform (Google Chrome, Safari, Microsoft explorer) and go to:

[https://cholstro.shinyapps.io/shiny-music/](https://cholstro.shinyapps.io/shiny-music/)

The app opens showing a welcome screen with stats about the database, 
including the total number of albums and artists.

---

## What the App Contains

The app has seven tabs across the top:

| Tab | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Welcome screen with database stats |
| **Number One Albums** | Albums that reached the top ranking each year |
| **Top Albums by Year** | The highest ranked albums for any year you select |
| **Artists' Albums** | All albums by a specific artist |
| **Favorite Artists** | Artists who appear most frequently in the rankings |
| **Artist Comparison** | Side-by-side ranking comparison of two artists |
| **Vinyl** | Albums in the database that are available on vinyl |

---

## Task 1: View Top Albums for a Specific Year

1. Click  **Top Albums by Year** tab.
2. Use dropdown menu to select a year.
3. Click **Submit**.

The table updates to show the highest ranked albums for that year, 
sorted from best to worst.

---

## Task 2: Look Up Albums by an Artist

1. Click the **Artist' Albums** tab.
2. Type or select an artist name from the dropdown.
3. Click **Submit**.

You see all albums by that artist in the database, along with their 
rankings and years.

---

## Task 3: Compare Two Artists

1. Click the **Artist Comparison** tab.
2. Select your first artist from the first dropdown.
3. Select your second artist from the second dropdown.
4. Click **Submit**.

The app displays ranking information for both artists side by side.

---

## Task 4: View Number One Albums

1. Click the **Number One Albums** tab.
2. Select a year from the dropdown.
3. Click **Submit**.

You see every album that reached the number one ranking position 
for that year.

---

## Task 5: Browse Vinyl Albums

1. Click the **Vinyl** tab.

You see all albums in the database that the owner has on vinyl.
